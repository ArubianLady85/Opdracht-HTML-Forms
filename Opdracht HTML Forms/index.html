The Netherlands back in a semi Lockdown

The Netherlands will be back on a semi-lockdown for the next 3 weeks. All non-essential shopd must close at 18:00 and all essential shops have to close at 20:00. You can only receive 4 people on a hoe visit. Mouth caps and 1,5m is also mandatory again. Not to forget, work from home as much as possible.

Curacao is also starting with QR-Codes

All tourists flying to Curacao must do a PCR-test again that shouldn't be older than 48 hours. 3 Days after arrival, all tourists have to take an extra rapid test to make sure they don't have Covid-19. If you want to go out on the island, you must have you QR-code ready to show. There is also still a curfew on the island.

Marokko tightens corona Measures

Morocco has also decided to start with extra strict checks on everyone who flies to Morocco. You must test immediately upon arrival, if you are positive, you must fly back at your own expense. If you have already tested, you must prove that your negative. More than half of the country has already been vaccinated against covid.
